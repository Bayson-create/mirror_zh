Therigatha Atthakatha — Commentary to the Therigatha《长老尼偈》义注——《长老尼偈》的注释
  • Therigatha Commentary (ThigA X.1) — Skinny Gotami and the Mustard Seed《长老尼偈》注（ThigA X.1）——瘦瞿昙弥与芥菜籽 [Olendzki]. In this famous parable a mother, blinded by grief over her dead son, asks the Buddha for medicine to cure her son's condition. The Buddha's cure works better than she could ever have imagined. （Olendzki 英译）这则著名的譬喻讲述一位因丧子之痛而心智迷乱的母亲，向佛陀求药医治亡子。佛陀所开的"药方"，其疗效远超她所能想象。
