The Threefold Refuge (The Triple Gem)三皈依（三宝）

These pages invite you to explore some of the Buddha's basic teachings as they are presented in the Pali canon. Each page in this section contains a selection of short passages from the suttas that introduce or illustrate different aspects of a single topic. If you encounter a particularly meaningful or interesting passage you can, in most cases, read the full text of the sutta from which it came by simply following the link at the end of that passage. Many of the passages are cross-referenced to other pages, allowing you to pursue a theme to whatever depth or breadth you desire. 本专题邀请你探索巴利三藏中所呈现的佛陀基本教法。本区各页均精选了若干出自经文的短篇段落，用以引介或阐明某一主题的不同面向。若遇到特别有意义或有趣的段落，通常只需点击段落末尾的链接，即可阅读该段落出处经文的完整内容。许多段落还与其他页面互相参照，方便你依个人兴趣深入或延展某一主题。

This is by no means an exhaustive tutorial. A number of the topics introduced here are explored more thoroughly in the Study Guides. The General Index also has many references to additional readings on related topics. 本专题并非详尽的教程。此处介绍的部分主题在"学习指南"中有更深入的探讨；"总索引"中也收录了许多相关延伸阅读的链接。

Begin your tour by exploring the Threefold Refuge (The Triple Gem):让我们从"三皈依"（三宝）开始这趟旅程：

  1. The Buddha佛：A sketch of the Buddha's life, based on excerpts from the suttas.基于经文选段的佛陀生平速写。
  2. The Dhamma法：An outline of the Buddha's teachings, organized according to his method of "gradual instruction" (anupubbi-katha). The Buddha frequently used this framework to guide his students from first principles through progressively more advanced teachings, all the way to the fulfillment of the Four Noble Truths and the realization of Nibbana.依佛陀"次第说法"（anupubbi-katha）的方式编排的教法纲要。佛陀常以此框架引导弟子由浅入深，从基本原理逐步深入，直至圆满四圣谛、证悟涅槃。
  3. The Sangha僧：Descriptions of the Sangha — the community of persons who have gained at least some degree of Awakening (ariya-sangha) and the community of ordained monks and nuns (bhikkhu-sangha and bhikkhuni-sangha).关于僧伽的说明——既指已证得某种程度觉悟的圣者团体（圣僧伽），也指出家受戒的比丘、比丘尼僧团。

Further reading:

延伸阅读：

  • Buddhist Dictionary, by Nyanatiloka Mahathera. A classic handbook of important terms and concepts in Theravada Buddhism.《佛教辞典》，向智大长老（Nyanatiloka Mahathera）著。上座部佛教重要术语与概念的经典手册。
  • Path to Deliverance, by Nyanatiloka Mahathera. A systematic exposition of essential points of Dhamma based on passages from the suttas.《解脱之道》，向智大长老著。以经文段落为基础，系统阐述法义要点。
  • Refuge: An Introduction to the Buddha, Dhamma, and Sangha by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.《皈依：佛法僧导论》，坦尼沙罗尊者（Thanissaro Bhikkhu）著。
  • Study Guides: anthologies of short passages suitable for individual and group study.学习指南：适合个人或团体研读的短文选集。
  • The Wings to Awakening: An Anthology from the Pali canon, by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.《通往觉悟之翼：巴利三藏选集》，坦尼沙罗尊者著。
