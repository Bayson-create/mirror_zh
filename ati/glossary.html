AA 部

Abhidhamma:阿毗达摩（论藏）：
(1) In the discourses of the Pali canon, this term simply means "higher Dhamma," and a systematic attempt to define the Buddha's teachings and understand their interrelationships. (2) A later collection of analytical treatises based on lists of categories drawn from the teachings in the discourses, added to the Canon several centuries after the Buddha's life. （1）在巴利经藏中，此词意为"更高的法"，指对佛陀教法及其相互关系加以系统界定的尝试。（2）指后世在佛灭数百年后才纳入三藏的一批分析性论著，其内容以经中教法所涉及的各类范畴为基础展开分析。
abhiññā:神通（abhiññā）：
Intuitive powers that come from the practice of concentration: the ability to display psychic powers, clairvoyance, clairaudience, the ability to know the thoughts of others, recollection of past lifetimes, and the knowledge that does away with mental effluents. 由修习禅定而生起的直观能力，包括：显现神通、天眼通、天耳通、他心通、宿命通，以及断尽诸漏的智慧（漏尽通）。
ācariya:阿闍梨（ācariya）：
Teacher; mentor. 老师；导师。
adhiṭṭhāna:决意（adhiṭṭhāna）：
Determination; resolution. One of the ten perfections (pāramīs). 决心；誓愿。十波罗蜜之一。
ajaan, ajahn, achaan, etc.:阿姜（泰语 ajaan/ajahn）：
(Thai). Teacher; mentor. Equivalent to the Pali ācariya. （泰语）老师；导师。相当于巴利语的 ācariya（阿闍梨）。
akāliko:不受时限（akāliko）：
Timeless; unconditioned by time or season. 超越时间；不受时节因缘所限。
akusala:不善（akusala）：
Unwholesome, unskillful, demeritorious. See its opposite, kusala. 不善；不巧妙；无功德的。其反义词见"kusala（善）"。
anāgāmī:阿那含（不还者，anāgāmī）：
Non-returner. A person who has abandoned the five lower fetters that bind the mind to the cycle of rebirth, and who after death will appear in one of the Brahma worlds called the Pure Abodes, there to attain nibbāna, never again to return to this world. 不还者。已断除将心系缚于轮回的五下分结的人，此人命终后将往生于称为"净居天"的梵天界，在那里证得涅槃，永不再返回此世。
ānāpānasati:安那般那念（入出息念，ānāpānasati）：
Mindfulness of breathing. A meditation practice in which one maintains one's attention and mindfulness on the sensations of breathing. 出入息念；一种将注意力与正念持续安住于呼吸感受上的禅修方法。
anattā:无我（anattā）：
Not-self; ownerless. 非我；无主的。
anicca:无常（anicca）：
Inconstant; unsteady; impermanent. 不恒常；不稳固；无常的。
anupādisesa-nibbāna:无余涅槃（anupādisesa-nibbāna）：
Nibbāna with no fuel remaining — the nibbāna of the arahant after his passing away. 无燃料残留的涅槃（喻如已熄之火，余烬亦冷）——阿罗汉命终后所证的涅槃。对照"有余涅槃（sa-upādisesa-nibbāna）"。
anupubbī-kathā:次第说法（anupubbī-kathā）：
Gradual instruction. The Buddha's method of teaching Dhamma that guides his listeners progressively through increasingly advanced topics: generosity, virtue, heavens, drawbacks, renunciation, and the four noble truths. 循序渐进的教导方式。佛陀说法时引导听者由浅入深，依次讲说布施、持戒、生天之乐、欲望过患、出离，最后讲说四圣谛。
anusaya:随眠（anusaya）：
Obsession; underlying tendency. There are seven major obsessions: sensual passion, resistance, views, uncertainty, conceit, passion for becoming, and ignorance. 潜伏的心理倾向；执着的根源。共有七种：欲贪随眠、瞋恚随眠、见随眠、疑随眠、慢随眠、有贪随眠、无明随眠。
apāya-bhūmi:恶趣（apāya-bhūmi）：
State of deprivation; the four lower levels of existence into which one might be reborn as a result of past unskillful actions: rebirth in hell, as a hungry ghost, as an angry demon, or as a common animal. None of these states is permanent. 匮乏之境；由过去不善业而可能投生的四种较低层次的存在：地狱、饿鬼、阿修罗、畜生。这些境界皆非永恒。
appamāda:不放逸（appamāda）：
Heedfulness; diligence; zeal. The cornerstone of all skillful mental states. 警觉；精进；不懈怠。一切善心状态的基石，佛陀临终遗教即以此为核心："诸行皆坏灭，当以不放逸成就道业。"
arahant:阿罗汉（arahant）：
A "worthy one" or "pure one"; a person whose mind is free of defilement, who has abandoned all ten of the fetters that bind the mind to the cycle of rebirth, whose heart is free of mental effluents, and who is thus not destined for further rebirth. "应供"或"清净者"；心已离垢染、断尽十结、心无诸漏、不再投生轮回之人。是佛陀的称号之一，也是其圣弟子所能证得的最高果位。
ārammaṇa:所缘（ārammaṇa）：
Preoccupation; mental object. 心的所缘对象；心念所专注之处。
ariya:圣（ariya）：
Noble, ideal. Also, a "Noble One." 高尚的、理想的。亦指"圣者"（ariya-puggala）。
ariyadhana:圣财（ariyadhana）：
Noble Wealth; qualities that serve as 'capital' in the quest for liberation: conviction, virtue, conscience, fear of evil, erudition, generosity, and discernment. 圣者的财富；作为解脱资粮的诸品质：信、戒、惭、愧、多闻、施、慧。
ariya-puggala:圣者（ariya-puggala）：
Noble person; enlightened individual. An individual who has realized at least the lowest of the four noble paths or their fruitions. 圣人；已证悟者。至少已证得四圣道（或其果位）中最初一种果位的人。对照"puthujjana（凡夫）"。
ariya-sacca:圣谛（ariya-sacca）：
Noble Truth. There are four: stress, the origin of stress, the disbanding of stress, and the path of practice leading to the disbanding of stress. 圣谛；共有四种：苦谛、集谛、灭谛、道谛。
āsava:漏（āsava）：
Mental effluent, pollutant, or fermentation. Four qualities — sensuality, views, becoming, and ignorance — that "flow out" of the mind and create the flood of the round of death and rebirth. 心的漏泄、污染或发酵。共有四种从心中"流出"的漏：欲漏、见漏、有漏、无明漏，形成生死轮回之流。
asubha:不净（asubha）：
Unattractiveness, loathsomeness, foulness. The Buddha recommends contemplation of this aspect of the body as an antidote to lust and complacency. 丑陋、可厌、污秽。佛陀教导观身不净，作为对治贪欲与懈怠的方法。
asura:阿修罗（asura）：
A race of beings who, like the Titans of Greek mythology, fought the devas for sovereignty over the heavens and lost. 一类有情，如希腊神话中的泰坦，曾与诸天（deva）争夺天界主权而落败。属恶趣之一。
avijjā:无明（avijjā）：
Unawareness; ignorance; obscured awareness; delusion about the nature of the mind. 不觉知；愚痴；对心之本质的迷惑不解。
āyatana:处（āyatana）：
Sense medium. The inner sense media are the sense organs: eyes, ears, nose, tongue, body, and mind. The outer sense media are their respective objects. 感官媒介；根与尘。内六处为六根：眼、耳、鼻、舌、身、意；外六处为其对应的六尘。

BB 部

bhante:尊者（bhante）：
Venerable sir; often used when addressing a Buddhist monk. 尊者；对佛教比丘的敬称。
bhava:有（bhava）：
Becoming. States of being that develop first in the mind and can then be experienced as internal worlds and/or as worlds on an external level. There are three levels of becoming: on the sensual level, the level of form, and the level of formlessness. 存在的生成过程；"有"。先于心中形成的存在状态，进而体验为内在与/或外在世界。共有三种层次：欲有、色有、无色有。
bhāvanā:修习（bhāvanā）：
Mental cultivation or development; meditation. The third of the three grounds for meritorious action. 心的培育与开发；禅修。三种福业事之第三种（另二为布施、持戒）。
bhikkhu:比丘（bhikkhu）：
A Buddhist monk; a man who has given up the householder's life to live a life of heightened virtue in accordance with the Vinaya in general, and the Pātimokkha rules in particular. 佛教比丘；舍弃居家生活、依律藏及波罗提木叉戒条而持守增上戒行的出家男众。
bhikkhunī:比丘尼（bhikkhunī）：
A Buddhist nun; a woman who has given up the householder's life to live a life of heightened virtue in accordance with the Vinaya in general, and the Pātimokkha rules in particular. 佛教比丘尼；舍弃居家生活、依律藏及波罗提木叉戒条而持守增上戒行的出家女众。
bodhi-pakkhiya-dhammā:菩提分法（bodhi-pakkhiya-dhammā）：
"Wings to Awakening" — seven sets of principles conducive to Awakening: [1] the four frames of reference (satipaṭṭhāna); [2] four right exertions; [3] four bases of success; [4] five dominant factors (indriya); [5] five strengths; [6] seven factors for Awakening; and [7] the eightfold path. "通往觉悟之翼"——佛陀教法核心的七类法门：（1）四念处；（2）四正勤；（3）四神足；（4）五根；（5）五力；（6）七觉支；（7）八正道。
bodhisatta:菩萨（bodhisatta）：
"A being (striving) for Awakening"; the term used to describe the Buddha before he actually became Buddha. Sanskrit form: Bodhisattva. "趋向觉悟者"；指佛陀成佛之前的称谓，梵文作"菩提萨埵"（Bodhisattva）。
brahmā:梵天（brahmā）：
"Great One" — an inhabitant of the non-sensual heavens of form or formlessness. "大者"——色界或无色界（非欲界）天的居住者。
brahma-vihāra:梵住／四无量心（brahma-vihāra）：
The four "sublime" or "divine" abodes that are attained through the development of boundless mettā (goodwill), karuṇā (compassion), muditā (appreciative joy), and upekkhā (equanimity). 四种"崇高"或"梵天"住处，由修习无量的慈（mettā）、悲（karuṇā）、喜（muditā）、舍（upekkhā）而证得。
brahman:婆罗门（brahman）：
The brahman caste of India has long maintained that its members, by their birth, are worthy of the highest respect. Buddhism borrowed this term to apply to those who have attained the goal, showing that respect is earned by spiritual attainment, not birth or caste. In the Buddhist sense, synonymous with arahant. 印度婆罗门种姓自古主张其成员因出身而理应受最高尊敬。佛教借用此词指称已证得目标者，藉此表明尊敬应由修行成就而非出身种姓赢得。在佛教用法中，此词与"阿罗汉"同义。
buddho:佛陀（buddho）：
Awake; enlightened. An epithet for the Buddha. 觉醒的；已开悟的。佛陀的称号之一。
Buddha:佛（Buddha）：
The name given to one who rediscovers for himself the liberating path of Dhamma, after a long period of its having been forgotten by the world. The most recent Buddha was born Siddhattha Gotama in India in the sixth century BCE. After seven years of austerities in the forest, he rediscovered the "middle way" and achieved his goal, becoming Buddha. 佛，指在正法长久被世间遗忘之后，独自重新发现解脱之道者。距今最近一位佛陀于公元前六世纪生于印度，本名悉达多·乔达摩。经七年林中苦行后，重新发现"中道"，证得目标而成佛。

CC 部

caṅkama:经行（caṅkama）：
Walking meditation, usually in the form of walking back and forth along a prescribed path. 行走禅修，通常在一段固定路径上来回踱步进行。
cetasika:心所（cetasika）：
Mental concomitant (see vedanā, saññā, and saṅkhāra). 心的伴随因素；心所法（参见受、想、行）。
citta:心（citta）：
Mind; heart; state of consciousness. 心；心识状态。

DD 部

dāna:布施（dāna）：
Giving, liberality; offering, alms. Dana is the first theme in the Buddha's system of gradual training, the first of the ten pāramīs, one of the seven treasures, and the first of the three grounds for meritorious action. 给予、慷慨；供养、布施。布施是佛陀次第教法的第一主题，十波罗蜜之首，七圣财之一，三种福业事之首。
deva; devatā:天神（deva；devatā）：
Literally, "shining one" — an inhabitant of the heavenly realms. 字面意为"发光者"——天界的居住者。
Devadatta:提婆达多（Devadatta）：
A cousin of the Buddha who tried to effect a schism in the saṅgha and who has since become emblematic for all Buddhists who work knowingly or unknowingly to undermine the religion from within. 佛陀的堂弟，曾图谋分裂僧团，此后成为一切明知或不自觉地从内部损害佛教之人的象征。
dhamma [Skt. dharma]:法（dhamma；梵语 dharma）：
(1) Event; a phenomenon in and of itself; (2) mental quality; (3) doctrine, teaching; (4) nibbāna. "Dhamma" (usu. capitalized) is used also to denote any doctrine that teaches such things. （1）事件；现象本身；（2）心的品质；（3）教理、教法；（4）涅槃。大写的"Dhamma"亦泛指教导此类内容的教理体系，如"佛法"兼指佛陀的教导与涅槃的亲证经验。
Dhamma-vinaya:法与律（Dhamma-vinaya）：
"Doctrine (dhamma) and discipline (vinaya)." The Buddha's own name for the religion he founded. "教法（dhamma）与戒律（vinaya）"——佛陀自己为其所创宗教所定的名称。
dhana:财（dhana）：
Treasure(s). The seven qualities of conviction, virtue, conscience & concern, learning, generosity, and wisdom. 财富、宝藏。七圣财：信、戒、惭愧、多闻、施、慧。
dhātu:界（dhātu）：
Element; property, impersonal condition. The four physical elements are earth, water, wind, and fire. The six elements include the above four plus space and consciousness. 元素；非人格化的条件。四大：地、水、火、风。六界为四大再加空与识。
dhutaṅga:头陀支（dhutaṅga）：
Voluntary ascetic practices that monks may undertake to cultivate renunciation and contentment. For monks, there are thirteen such practices, including using only patched-up robes, going for alms, eating no more than one meal a day, living in the forest, and not lying down. 比丘可自愿受持的苦行支，用以培养出离与知足，共十三支，如着粪扫衣、日中一食、林居、常坐不卧等。
dosa:瞋（dosa）：
Aversion; hatred; anger. One of three unwholesome roots in the mind. 厌恶；嗔恨；愤怒。心中三不善根之一。
dukkha:苦（dukkha）：
Stress; suffering; pain; distress; discontent. 苦；压力；痛苦；不满足。

EE 部

effluents:诸漏：
See āsava.见"āsava（漏）"。
ekaggatārammaṇa:心一境性（ekaggatārammaṇa）：
Singleness of preoccupation; "one-pointedness." Reaches full maturity upon the development of the fourth level of jhāna. 专注对象的单一性；"一心专注"。于第四禅时臻于成熟。
ekāyana-magga:一乘道（ekāyana-magga）：
A unified path; a direct path. An epithet for the practice of being mindful of the four frames of reference: body, feelings, mind, and mental qualities. 统一之道；直接之道。指修习四念处（身、受、心、法）之正念的别称。
evaṃ:如是（evaṃ）：
Thus; in this way. Used in Thailand as a formal closing to a sermon. 如此；这样。在泰国用作说法结束时的正式结语。

frame of reference:所缘的范畴：

GG 部

gotrabhū-ñāṇa:种姓智（gotrabhū-ñāṇa）：
"Change of lineage knowledge": The glimpse of nibbāna that changes one from an ordinary person to a Noble One. "转种姓智"：瞥见涅槃、使凡夫转为圣者的关键智慧。

HH 部

Hīnayāna:小乘（Hīnayāna）：
"Inferior Vehicle," originally a pejorative term coined by followers of the Mahāyāna to denote the path of those who adhered only to the earliest discourses. The Theravāda school of today is a descendent of the Hīnayāna. "低劣之乘"，原为大乘（Mahāyāna，"大乘"）追随者所创的贬义词，用以指称只承认最早期经典为佛语的修行者。今日的上座部（Theravāda）即源自小乘一系。
hiri-ottappa:惭愧（hiri-ottappa）：
"Conscience and concern"; "moral shame and moral dread." These twin emotions — the "guardians of the world" — are associated with all skillful actions. "良心与顾虑"；"道德羞耻与道德畏惧"。此二者被称为"护世法"，与一切善行相应。惭（hiri）是不愿有损自尊而生的内在良知；愧（ottappa）是对不善行可能招致自他伤害的健康畏惧。

II 部

idappaccayatā:此缘性（idappaccayatā）：
This/that conditionality. This name for the causal principle the Buddha discovered on the night of his Awakening stresses that causality can be understood entirely in terms of forces and conditions experienced directly, without reference to forces outside that realm. "此有故彼有"的条件性；佛陀于觉悟之夜所发现的因果法则，强调因果历程完全可依直接经验中的诸力与条件来理解，无需诉诸经验之外的力量。
indriya:根（indriya）：
Faculties; mental factors. Can refer either to the six sense media or to the five mental factors of saddhā (conviction), viriya (persistence), sati (mindfulness), samādhi (concentration), and paññā (discernment). 诸根；心的能力。可指六根（六种感官媒介），或指信、精进、念、定、慧五根。

JJ 部

jhāna [Skt. dhyāna]:禅那（jhāna；梵语 dhyāna）：
Mental absorption. A state of strong concentration focused on a single physical sensation or mental notion. Development of jhāna arises from the temporary suspension of the five hindrances through the development of five mental factors: vitakka, vicāra, pīti, sukha, and ekaggatārammaṇa. 禅定；心的专注安止。是聚焦于单一身心所缘的深度专注状态，透过五禅支（寻、伺、喜、乐、心一境性）的培育而暂时止息五盖。

KK 部

kalyāṇamitta:善友（kalyāṇamitta）：
Admirable friend; a mentor or teacher of Dhamma. 善知识；法的导师或友伴。
kāmaguṇa:欲乐（kāmaguṇa）：
Strings of sensuality. The objects of the five physical senses: visible objects, sounds, aromas, flavors, and tactile sensations. "感官之弦"；五种感官对象：色、声、香、味、触。因如琴弦拨动能生悦意之受而得名。
kamma [Skt. karma]:业（kamma；梵语 karma）：
Intentional acts that result in states of being and birth. 导致存在与投生状态的有意行为。
kammaṭṭhāna:业处（kammaṭṭhāna）：
Literally, "basis of work." Refers to the meditation themes by which defilement, craving, and ignorance may be uprooted. By extension, includes all forty classical meditation themes. Also used to identify the Thai forest tradition founded by Phra Ajaan Mun and Phra Ajaan Sao. 字面意为"工作的基础"；指用以根除烦恼、渴爱、无明的禅修主题，引申指四十种传统禅修业处的总称。此词也用来指称由阿姜曼与阿姜绍所创立的泰国森林传承。
karuṇā:悲（karuṇā）：
Compassion; sympathy; the aspiration to find a way to be truly helpful to oneself and others. One of the four "sublime abodes." 悲悯；同情；愿真实利益自他之心。四梵住之一。
kaṭhina:迦絺那（kaṭhina）：
A ceremony, held in the fourth month of the rainy season, in which a saṅgha receives a gift of cloth from lay people, and makes it into a robe before dawn of the following day. 功德衣仪式；在雨安居第四个月举行，僧团接受居士所供之布料，并须于次日黎明前制成袈裟。
kāya:身（kāya）：
Body. Usually refers to the physical body, but sometimes refers to the mental body. 身体。通常指色身，有时也指名身（意的聚合）。
kāyagatā-sati:身至念（kāyagatā-sati）：
Mindfulness immersed in the body. Covers several meditation themes: keeping the breath in mind, being mindful of posture and activities, analyzing the body into parts and physical properties, and contemplating that the body is subject to death. 于身安住正念；涵盖多种禅修主题，包括觉知呼吸、觉知姿势与动作、分析身体各部位与四大元素、观身必将坏灭等。
khandha:蕴（khandha）：
Heap; group; aggregate. Physical and mental components of the personality and of sensory experience. The five bases of clinging: nāma (mental), rūpa (physical), vedanā (feeling), saññā (perception), saṅkhāra (mental fashionings), and viññāṇa (consciousness). 聚合、蕴。构成人格与感官经验的身心组成部分，即五取蕴：色（物质现象）、受（感受）、想（认知）、行（心的造作）、识（了别）。
khanti:忍辱（khanti）：
Patience; forbearance. One of the ten perfections. 耐心；忍受。十波罗蜜之一。
kilesa:烦恼（kilesa）：
Defilement — lobha (passion), dosa (aversion), and moha (delusion) in their various forms, including greed, malevolence, anger, hypocrisy, arrogance, envy, dishonesty, pride, and complacency. 染污；贪、瞋、痴三者的各种表现形式，包括贪婪、恶意、愤怒、虚伪、傲慢、嫉妒、不诚实、我慢、自满等。
kusala:善（kusala）：
Wholesome, skillful, good, meritorious. An action characterized by this quality is bound to result eventually in happiness. Its opposite (akusala) leads to sorrow. 善的；巧妙的；具功德的。此性质的行为终将导致安乐，其反面（不善）则导致苦恼。

LL 部

lakkhaṇa:相（lakkhaṇa）：
See ti-lakkhaṇa.见"ti-lakkhaṇa（三相）"。
lobha:贪（lobha）：
Greed; passion; unskillful desire. Also rāga. One of three unwholesome roots in the mind. 贪婪；欲望；不善欲求。亦作"rāga"。心中三不善根之一。
loka-dhamma:世间法（loka-dhamma）：
Affairs or phenomena of the world. The standard list gives eight: wealth, loss of wealth, status, loss of status, praise, criticism, pleasure, and pain. 世间的境遇或现象；常说有八种（八风）：利、衰、称、讥、誉、毁、苦、乐。
lokavidū:世间解（lokavidū）：
Knower of the cosmos. An epithet for the Buddha. 洞悉世间者。佛陀的称号之一。
lokuttara:出世间（lokuttara）：
Transcendent; supramundane. 超越世间的；出世间的。

MM 部

magga:道（magga）：
Path. Specifically, the path to the cessation of suffering and stress: the path to stream-entry, once-returning, non-returning, and arahantship. 道；特指导向苦之止息的道路：入流道、一来道、不还道、阿罗汉道。
mahāthera:大长老（mahāthera）：
"Great elder." An honorific title automatically conferred upon a bhikkhu of at least twenty years' standing. "大长老"；受戒满二十年以上的比丘自动获得的尊称。对照"thera（长老）"。
majjhimā:中（majjhimā）：
Middle; appropriate; just right. 中间的；恰当的；刚刚好的。
Māra:魔罗（Māra）：
The personification of evil and temptation. 邪恶与诱惑的人格化象征。
mettā:慈（mettā）：
Loving-kindness; goodwill. One of the ten perfections and one of the four "sublime abodes." 慈爱；善意。十波罗蜜之一，四梵住之一。
moha:痴（moha）：
Delusion; ignorance. One of three unwholesome roots in the mind. 愚痴；无明。心中三不善根之一。
muditā:喜（muditā）：
Appreciative/sympathetic joy. Taking delight in one's own goodness and that of others. One of the four "sublime abodes." 随喜；对自他善行心生喜悦。四梵住之一。
mūla:根（mūla）：
Literally, "root." The fundamental conditions that determine the moral quality of one's intentional actions. The three unskillful roots are lobha (greed), dosa (aversion), and moha (delusion); the skillful roots are their opposites. 字面意为"根"；决定意业道德性质的根本条件。三不善根为贪、瞋、痴；三善根则与之相反。

NN 部

nāga:龙（nāga）：
A term commonly used to refer to strong, stately, and heroic animals, such as elephants and magical serpents. In Buddhism, also used to refer to those who have attained the goal of the practice. 常用以指称强壮、庄严、英勇的动物，如大象与神话中的巨蛇。在佛教中，也用以指称已证得修行目标之人。
nāma:名（nāma）：
Mental phenomena. A collective term for vedanā (feeling), saññā (perception), cetanā (intention), phassa (sensory contact) and manasikāra (attention). Compare rūpa. 心理现象；受、想、思（意志）、触（感官接触）、作意（注意）的总称。对照"rūpa（色）"。
nāma-rūpa:名色（nāma-rūpa）：
Name-and-form; mind-and-matter. The union of mental phenomena and physical phenomena, conditioned by consciousness in the causal chain of dependent co-arising. 名与色；心理现象与物质现象的结合，在缘起法则中由识所缘生。
nekkhamma:出离（nekkhamma）：
Renunciation; literally, "freedom from sensual lust." One of the ten pāramīs. 出离；字面意为"离欲贪之自由"。十波罗蜜之一。
nibbāna [Skt. nirvāṇa]:涅槃（nibbāna；梵语 nirvāṇa）：
Liberation; literally, the "unbinding" of the mind from mental effluents, defilements, and the round of rebirth. As this term also denotes the extinguishing of a fire, it carries connotations of stilling, cooling, and peace. 解脱；字面意为心从诸漏、烦恼与轮回中"解开束缚"。此词亦指火焰的熄灭，故带有止息、清凉、寂静的意涵。
nibbidā:厌离（nibbidā）：
Disenchantment; aversion; disgust; weariness. The skillful turning-away of the mind from the conditioned world towards the unconditioned — nibbāna. 厌倦；厌离。心善巧地由有为的轮回世界转向无为的涅槃。
nimitta:相／禅相（nimitta）：
Mental sign, image, or vision that may arise in meditation. 禅修中可能生起的心相、影像或异象。
nirodha:灭（nirodha）：
Cessation; disbanding; stopping. 止息；消散；停止。
nīvaraṇa:盖（nīvaraṇa）：
Hindrances to concentration — sensual desire, ill will, sloth & drowsiness, restlessness & anxiety, and uncertainty. 障碍定力的五盖：欲贪、瞋恚、昏沉睡眠、掉举恶作、疑。

OO 部

opanayiko:引导向内（opanayiko）：
Referring inwardly; to be brought inward. An epithet for the Dhamma. 导向内在；应向内体证。法的称号之一。

PQPQ 部

pabbajjā:出家（pabbajjā）：
"Going forth (from home to the homeless life)"; ordination as a sāmaṇera (sāmaṇerī), or novice monk (nun). "由家至无家的出离"；受戒成为沙弥（沙弥尼）。
paccattaṃ:各别自知（paccattaṃ）：
Personal; individual.个人的；各自的。
paccekabuddha:辟支佛（paccekabuddha）：
Private Buddha. One who, like a Buddha, has gained Awakening without the benefit of a teacher, but who lacks the requisite pāramīs to teach others. 独觉佛。如佛陀般无师自证觉悟，但缺乏教导他人所需圆满波罗蜜之人，证悟后过独居生活。
Pāḷi:巴利语（Pāḷi）：
The canon of texts preserved by the Theravāda school and, by extension, the language in which those texts are composed. 上座部所传承的三藏典籍，引申指该典籍所使用的语言。
paññā:慧（paññā）：
Discernment; insight; wisdom; intelligence; common sense. One of the ten perfections. 辨识力；洞察；智慧。十波罗蜜之一。
papañca:戏论（papañca）：
Complication, proliferation, objectification. The tendency of the mind to proliferate issues from the sense of "self." 概念的增殖、扩散；心从"我"之感受中不断衍生纷杂念头的倾向。
pāramī, pāramitā:波罗蜜（pāramī，pāramitā）：
Perfection of the character. Ten qualities developed by a bodhisatta over many lifetimes: generosity, virtue, renunciation, discernment, energy, patience, truthfulness, determination, good will, and equanimity. 品格的圆满；菩萨历经多生累劫所培育的十种德行：布施、持戒、出离、智慧、精进、忍辱、真实、决意、慈、舍。
parinibbāna:般涅槃（parinibbāna）：
Total Unbinding; the complete cessation of the khandhas that occurs upon the death of an arahant. 究竟解脱；阿罗汉命终时五蕴的彻底止息。
parisā:四众（parisā）：
Following; assembly. The four groups of the Buddha's following: monks, nuns, laymen, and laywomen. 追随者；集会。佛陀的四众弟子：比丘、比丘尼、优婆塞、优婆夷。
pariyatti:教理（pariyatti）：
Theoretical understanding of Dhamma obtained through reading, study, and learning. 透过阅读、研习而获得的对法的理论理解。
paṭicca-samuppāda:缘起（paṭicca-samuppāda）：
Dependent co-arising; dependent origination. A map showing how the aggregates and sense media interact with ignorance and craving to bring about stress and suffering. 缘起；说明诸蕴与诸处如何与无明、渴爱相互作用而带来苦的法则图示。
Pātimokkha:波罗提木叉（Pātimokkha）：
The basic code of monastic discipline, consisting of 227 rules for monks and 311 for nuns. 出家戒律的基本规范，比丘戒227条，比丘尼戒311条。
paṭipadā:行道（paṭipadā）：
Road, path, way. The "Middle way" taught by the Buddha; the path of practice described in the fourth noble truth. 道路、途径；佛陀所教的"中道"，即四圣谛中道谛所述之修行道路。
paṭipatti:修行（paṭipatti）：
The practice of Dhamma, as opposed to mere theoretical knowledge. 法的实修，有别于单纯的理论认知（教理）。
paṭivedha:通达（paṭivedha）：
Direct, first-hand realization of the Dhamma. 对法的直接亲证。
peta [Skt. preta]:饿鬼（peta；梵语 preta）：
A "hungry shade" or "hungry ghost" — one of a class of beings in the lower realms, sometimes capable of appearing to human beings. "饥饿之影"或"饿鬼"——恶趣众生之一，有时能现身于人前。
phala:果（phala）：
Fruition. Specifically, the fruition of any of the four transcendent paths. 果位；特指四种出世间道之果。
phra:帕（泰语 phra）：
(Thai). Venerable. Used as a prefix to the name of a monk. （泰语）尊者；作为比丘名字前的敬称前缀。
pīti:喜（pīti）：
Rapture; bliss; delight. A pleasurable quality that reaches full maturity upon the development of the second level of jhāna. 喜悦；狂喜。禅修中于第二禅时臻于成熟的愉悦心质。
pūjā:供养（pūjā）：
Honor; respect; devotional observance. The devotional observances conducted at monasteries daily, on uposatha days, or on other special occasions. 恭敬；礼拜。寺院每日晨昏、布萨日或特殊场合所举行的礼敬仪式。
puñña:福（puñña）：
Merit; worth; the inner sense of well-being that comes from having acted rightly or well. 功德；因行善而生起的内在安适感，能使人持续行善。
puthujjana:凡夫（puthujjana）：
One of the many-folk; a "worlding" or run-of-the-mill person. An ordinary person who has not yet realized any of the four stages of Awakening. 众多者之一；世俗凡人。尚未证得四种觉悟果位中任一者。对照"ariya-puggala（圣者）"。

rūpa:色（rūpa）：
Body; physical phenomenon; sense datum. Basic meaning is "appearance" or "form." As one of the khandhas, refers to physical phenomena or sensations. Also used opposed to nāma (mental phenomena). 身；物质现象；感官材料。基本含义为"外观"或"形态"。作为五蕴之一，指物质现象或感受；也用以与"名"（心理现象）相对而言。

SS 部

sabhāva-dhamma:自性法（sabhāva-dhamma）：
Condition of nature; any phenomenon, event, property, or quality as experienced in and of itself. 自然的状态；任何现象、事件、属性或性质，就其自身被体验的样貌。
sacca:谛（sacca）：
Truthfulness. One of the ten perfections.真实；诚实。十波罗蜜之一。
saddhā:信（saddhā）：
Conviction, faith. A confidence in the Buddha that gives one the willingness to put his teachings into practice. Becomes unshakeable upon the attainment of stream-entry. 信心；信仰。对佛陀的信任，使人愿意将其教法付诸实践。于证得入流果时变得不可动摇。
sādhu:善哉（sādhu）：
(exclamation) "It is well"; an expression showing appreciation or agreement. （感叹词）"善哉"；表示赞叹或认同之语。
sagga:天界（sagga）：
Heaven, heavenly realm. The dwelling place of the devas. Rebirth in the heavens is a reward for practicing generosity and virtue. Like all waystations in saṃsāra, rebirth here is temporary. 天界；诸天神所居之处。投生天界是修习布施与持戒的果报之一，但如轮回中一切驿站般，此境亦非永恒。
sakadāgāmī:斯陀含（一来者，sakadāgāmī）：
Once-returner. A person who has abandoned the first three fetters, has weakened the fetters of sensual passion and resistance, and after death is destined to be reborn in this world only once more. 一来者。已断三结、并淡薄欲贪与瞋恚二结之人，命终后至多仅须再返此世一次。
sakkāya-diṭṭhi:身见（sakkāya-diṭṭhi）：
Self-identification view. The view that mistakenly identifies any of the khandha as "self"; the first of the ten fetters. Its abandonment is a hallmark of stream-entry. 错将五蕴中任一蕴认作"我"的见解；十结中的第一结。断除身见是证得入流果的标志之一。
Sākyamuni:释迦牟尼（Sākyamuni）：
"Sage of the Sakyans"; an epithet for the Buddha."释迦族的圣者"；佛陀的称号之一。
sākya-putta:释子（sākya-putta）：
Son of the Sakyan. An epithet for Buddhist monks, the Buddha having been a native of the Sakyan Republic. "释迦之子"；因佛陀出身释迦族共和国，故以此称呼佛教比丘。
sallekha-dhamma:损减法（sallekha-dhamma）：
Topics of effacement (effacing defilement) — having few wants, being content, seclusion, persistence, virtue, concentration, discernment, release. "减损烦恼"的诸法门——少欲、知足、独处、精进、持戒、定、慧、解脱等。
samādhi:定（samādhi）：
Concentration; the practice of centering the mind in a single sensation or preoccupation, usually to the point of jhāna. 禅定；将心专注安住于单一所缘的修习，通常导向禅那。
samaṇa:沙门（samaṇa）：
Contemplative. Literally, a person who abandons the conventional obligations of social life in order to find a way of life more "in tune" with the ways of nature. 修行者；字面意为舍离世俗社会义务、寻求与自然之道更相应之生活方式的人。
sāmaṇera (sāmaṇerī):沙弥（沙弥尼，sāmaṇera / sāmaṇerī）：
Literally, a small samaṇa; a novice monk (nun) who observes ten precepts and who is a candidate for admission to the order of bhikkhus (bhikkhunīs). 字面意为"小沙门"；持守十戒、作为受具足戒预备者的见习僧尼。
sambhavesin:求生有情（sambhavesin）：
(A being) searching for a place to take birth.正寻求投生之处的有情。
sammuti:世俗谛（sammuti）：
Conventional reality; convention; relative truth; supposition; anything conjured into being by the mind. 世俗实在；约定俗成；相对真理；由心所构设之一切事物。
sampajañña:正知（sampajañña）：
Alertness; self-awareness; presence of mind; clear comprehension. 警觉；自我觉知；心的当下临在；清晰的了知。
saṃsāra:轮回（saṃsāra）：
Transmigration; the round of death and rebirth.流转；生死轮回之循环。
saṃvega:悚惧感（saṃvega）：
The oppressive sense of shock, dismay, and alienation that comes with realizing the futility of life as normally lived; a chastening sense of one's own complacency; an anxious urgency to find a way out. 因洞察世俗生活之虚妄而生的震惊、沮丧与疏离感；对自身长久盲目自满的警醒；急切寻求出离之道的紧迫感。
saṃyojana:结（saṃyojana）：
Fetter that binds the mind to the cycle of rebirth — self-identification views, uncertainty, grasping at precepts and practices, sensual passion, resistance, passion for form, passion for formless phenomena, conceit, restlessness, and unawareness. 将心系缚于轮回的十结：身见、疑、戒禁取、欲贪、瞋恚、色贪、无色贪、慢、掉举、无明。
sandiṭṭhiko:现见（sandiṭṭhiko）：
Self-evident; immediately apparent; visible here and now. An epithet for the Dhamma. 自明的；当下可见的。法的称号之一。
saṅgha:僧伽（saṅgha）：
On the conventional level, this term denotes the communities of Buddhist monks and nuns; on the ideal level, it denotes followers who have attained at least stream-entry. 在世俗层面，指比丘、比丘尼僧团；在理想（圣者）层面，指至少已证入流果的佛陀追随者，无论出家在家。
saṅkhāra:行（saṅkhāra）：
Formation, compound, fashioning, fabrication — the forces and factors that fashion things, the process of fashioning, and the fashioned things that result. 造作、行；塑造事物（物质或心理）的诸力与因素，及此塑造过程与其结果。作为五蕴之一时特指心中的诸种造作。
saññā:想（saññā）：
Label; perception; allusion; act of memory or recognition; interpretation. 标记；知觉；记忆与辨识的作用；诠释。
sanyojana:结（异写，sanyojana）：
See saṃyojana.见"saṃyojana（结）"。
sāsana:教法（sāsana）：
Literally, "message." The dispensation, doctrine, and legacy of the Buddha; the Buddhist religion. 字面意为"讯息"；佛陀的教说、教理与遗教；佛教。
sati:念（sati）：
Mindfulness, self-collectedness, powers of reference and retention. 正念；心的收摄自持；忆持与关照的能力。
satipaṭṭhāna:念处（satipaṭṭhāna）：
Foundation of mindfulness; frame of reference — body, feelings, mind, and mental events, viewed in and of themselves as they occur. 念的立足处；四念处——身、受、心、法，就其发生当下如实观照。
sa-upādisesa-nibbāna:有余涅槃（sa-upādisesa-nibbāna）：
Nibbāna with fuel remaining (the analogy is to an extinguished fire whose embers are still glowing) — liberation as experienced in this lifetime by an arahant. 尚有余燃的涅槃（喻如已熄之火而余烬仍热）——阿罗汉在世时所证的解脱。对照"无余涅槃"。
sāvaka:声闻（sāvaka）：
Literally, "hearer." A disciple of the Buddha, especially a noble disciple. 字面意为"听闻者"；佛陀的弟子，尤指圣弟子。
sayadaw:西亚多（缅甸语 sayadaw）：
(Burmese). Venerable teacher; an honorific title for a senior or eminent bhikkhu. （缅甸语）尊师；对资深或德高比丘的敬称。
sekha:有学（sekha）：
A "learner" or "one in training"; a noble disciple who has not yet attained arahantship. "学人"；尚未证得阿罗汉果的圣弟子。
sīla:戒（sīla）：
Virtue, morality. The quality that prevents one from falling away from the eightfold path. Sīla is the second theme in the gradual training, one of the ten pāramīs, and the second of the seven treasures. 德行；道德。防止偏离八正道的品质；持戒是次第教法的第二主题，十波罗蜜之一，七圣财之第二。
sīma:结界（sīma）：
Boundary or territory within which the monastic saṅgha's formal acts must be performed in order to be valid. 僧团正式羯磨（如受戒、诵戒、断诤等）须于其内进行方为有效的界域。
sotāpanna:须陀洹（入流者，sotāpanna）：
Stream winner. A person who has abandoned the first three fetters and has thus entered the "stream" flowing to nibbāna, ensuring rebirth at most seven more times. 入流者。已断三结、进入必将流向涅槃之"流"的人，至多仅须再投生七次，且仅生于人道或更高界。
stream-entry, stream-winner:入流、入流者：

stress:压力／苦：

stupa (Pali thūpa):窣堵波／塔（巴利语 thūpa）：
Originally, a burial mound enshrining relics of a holy person. Over the centuries this developed into the spired monuments familiar in Theravāda countries, and into the pagodas of East Asia. 原指供奉圣者（如佛陀）舍利的坟冢式建筑，历经演变成为上座部国家常见的尖顶塔形建筑，以及东亚的宝塔。

sugati:善趣（sugati）：
Happy destinations; the two higher levels of existence into which one might be reborn as a result of past skillful actions: rebirth in the human world or in the heavens. None of these states is permanent. 安乐的去处；由过去善业而可能投生的两种较高层次存在：人间或天界。此二境亦非永恒。
sugato:善逝（sugato）：

sukha:乐（sukha）：

sutta [Skt. sūtra]:经（sutta；梵语 sūtra）：
Literally, "thread"; a discourse or sermon by the Buddha or his contemporary disciples. More than 10,000 suttas are collected in the Sutta Piṭaka, widely regarded as the earliest record of the Buddha's teachings. 字面意为"线"；佛陀或其同时代弟子的开示或说法。经藏中收录逾万篇经文，被公认为佛陀教法最早的记录。

TT 部

tādi:如是（tādi）：
"Such," an adjective to describe one who has attained the goal. Indicates that the person's state is indefinable but not subject to change or influences of any sort. "如是"；形容已证得目标之人的形容词，表示其境界不可定义、亦不受任何变化或影响所动。
taṇhā:渴爱（taṇhā）：
Craving — for sensuality, for becoming, or for not-becoming. 渴爱——欲爱、有爱、无有爱（渴求存在或渴求不存在）。
tāpas:苦行热（tāpas）：
The purifying "heat" of meditative practice.禅修所生具净化作用的"热能"。
Tathāgata:如来（Tathāgata）：
Literally, "one who has truly gone" or "one who has become authentic," an epithet for a person who has attained the highest spiritual goal. Usually denotes the Buddha. 字面意为"如实而去者"或"已臻真实者"，古印度用以称呼已证得最高精神目标之人，佛教中通常专指佛陀。
than, tan:坦（泰语 than / tan）：
(Thai). Reverend, venerable.（泰语）尊敬的；尊者。
thera:长老（thera）：
"Elder." An honorific title automatically conferred upon a bhikkhu of at least ten years' standing. "长老"；受戒满十年以上的比丘自动获得的尊称。对照"mahāthera（大长老）"。
Theravāda:上座部（Theravāda）：
The "Doctrine of the Elders" — the only one of the early schools of Buddhism to have survived into the present; currently the dominant form of Buddhism in Thailand, Sri Lanka, and Burma. "长老之教说"——早期佛教部派中唯一延续至今者，目前是泰国、斯里兰卡、缅甸的主流佛教传统。
ti-lakkhaṇa:三相（ti-lakkhaṇa）：
Three characteristics inherent in all conditioned phenomena — being inconstant, stressful, and not-self. 一切有为法所具的三种共相：无常、苦、无我。
tipiṭaka [Skt. tripiṭaka]:三藏（tipiṭaka；梵语 tripiṭaka）：
The Buddhist (Pali) Canon. Literally, "three baskets": the Vinaya Piṭaka (disciplinary rules); Sutta Piṭaka (discourses); and Abhidhamma Piṭaka (abstract philosophical treatises). 佛教（巴利）圣典。字面意为"三个篮子"：律藏（戒律）、经藏（教说）、论藏（哲理性分析论著）。
tiratana:三宝（tiratana）：
The "Triple Gem" consisting of the Buddha, Dhamma, and Saṅgha — ideals to which all Buddhists turn for refuge. "三宝"，即佛、法、僧——一切佛教徒皈依的对象。
tisaraṇa:三皈依（tisaraṇa）：
The "Threefold Refuge" — the Buddha, Dhamma, and Saṅgha. "三重皈依"——佛、法、僧。

UU 部

uggahitaññu:利根者（uggahitaññu）：
Of swift understanding. The Buddha perceived four categories of beings: those of swift understanding, those needing lengthy explanation, those needing to be led through practice, and those who at best gain only verbal understanding. 悟性敏捷者。佛陀观察到四类有情：闻略即悟者、闻广方悟者、须经引导修习方悟者，以及至多只能获得文字理解者。
Unbinding:解开束缚：
See nibbāna.见"nibbāna（涅槃）"。
upādāna:取（upādāna）：
Clinging; attachment; sustenance for becoming and birth — attachment to sensuality, to views, to precepts and practices, and to theories of the self. 执取；对"有"与"生"起滋养作用的攀附：欲取、见取、戒禁取、我语取。
upasampadā:具足戒（upasampadā）：
Acceptance; full ordination as a bhikkhu or bhikkhunī.认可、纳受；比丘或比丘尼的正式受戒。
upāsaka/upāsikā:优婆塞／优婆夷（upāsaka／upāsikā）：
A male/female lay follower of the Buddha.佛陀的在家男／女追随者。
upekkhā:舍（upekkhā）：
Equanimity. One of the ten perfections and one of the four "sublime abodes." 平等心；舍心。十波罗蜜之一，四梵住之一。
uposatha:布萨（uposatha）：
Observance day, corresponding to the phases of the moon, on which lay people gather to listen to the Dhamma and observe special precepts; monks assemble to recite the Pātimokkha. 依月相而定的持戒日；在家众于此日聚集闻法并持特别戒律，比丘众则于新月、满月布萨日诵戒。

VV 部

vassa:雨安居（vassa）：
Rains Retreat. A period from July to October in which each monk is required to live settled in a single place and not wander freely about. 雨季安居；约七月至十月间，比丘须定居一处、不得随意游行的时期。
vaṭṭa:轮转（vaṭṭa）：
The cycle of birth, death, and rebirth. Denotes both the death and rebirth of living beings and the death and rebirth of defilement within the mind. 生死轮转之环；既指有情的生死流转，也指心中烦恼的生灭循环。
vedanā:受（vedanā）：
Feeling — pleasure (ease), pain (stress), or neither pleasure nor pain. 感受——乐受、苦受、不苦不乐受。
Vesak, Vesakha, Visakha, Wesak, etc. (visākha):卫塞节（visākha）：
The ancient name for the Indian lunar month in spring. Tradition holds the Buddha's birth, Awakening, and Parinibbāna each took place on the full-moon night of this month, commemorated annually. 印度古历中对应春季的月份名。相传佛陀的诞生、成道、涅槃皆发生于此月满月夜，故上座部佛教世界每年于此日举行卫塞节庆典纪念。
vicāra:伺（vicāra）：
Evaluation; sustained thought. The mental factor that allows attention to shift and move about in relation to the chosen meditation object. 伺察；持续的思维。使注意力得以在所缘对象上游移、评估的心所。
vijjā:明（vijjā）：
Clear knowledge; genuine awareness; science (specifically, the cognitive powers developed through concentration and discernment). 明晰之知；真实的觉知；由定慧所培育出的认知能力。
vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno:明行足（vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno）：
Consummate in knowledge and conduct. An epithet for the Buddha. 明与行皆圆满具足者。佛陀的称号之一。
vimutti:解脱（vimutti）：
Release; freedom from the fabrications and conventions of the mind. The suttas distinguish discernment-release (paññā-vimutti) and awareness-release (ceto-vimutti). 解脱；心从造作与俗谛中释放。经中区分"慧解脱"（paññā-vimutti）与"心解脱"（ceto-vimutti）。
Vinaya:律（Vinaya）：
The monastic discipline whose rules and traditions define every aspect of the bhikkhus' and bhikkhunīs' way of life. Its essence is contained in the Pātimokkha. 规范比丘、比丘尼生活各方面的僧团戒律，其核心内容收录于波罗提木叉。
viññāṇa:识（viññāṇa）：
Consciousness; cognizance; the act of taking note of sense data and ideas as they occur. 识；了别；对感官材料与念头生起当下的觉知作用。
vipāka:异熟（vipāka）：
The consequence and result of a past volitional action.过去意业行为所招致的果报。
vipassanā:观（毗婆舍那，vipassanā）：
Clear intuitive insight into physical and mental phenomena as they arise and disappear, seeing them in terms of the three characteristics and the four noble truths. 对身心现象生灭当下的清晰直观洞察，依三相与四圣谛如实照见诸法本质。
vipassanūpakkilesa:观染（vipassanūpakkilesa）：
Corruption of insight; intense experiences in meditation that can lead one to believe the path is complete. Ten types: light, psychic knowledge, rapture, serenity, pleasure, extreme conviction, excessive effort, obsession, indifference, and contentment. 观智的染污；禅修中可能生起、令人误以为已证道的强烈体验，共十种：光明、神通智、喜、轻安、乐、胜解、过度精进、执着、无所谓、满足。
viriya:精进（viriya）：
Persistence; energy. One of the ten perfections, the five strengths, and the five dominant factors. 坚持；活力。十波罗蜜之一，五力与五根之一。
vitakka:寻（vitakka）：
Directed thought. The mental factor by which one's attention is applied to the chosen meditation object. 寻思；将注意力投向所缘对象的心所。

WXYZWXYZ 部

yakkha:夜叉（yakkha）：
One of a special class of powerful "non-human" beings — sometimes kindly, sometimes murderous and cruel — corresponding roughly to the fairies and ogres of Western fairy tales. 一类特殊而强大的"非人"有情，时而慈善、时而凶残，大致相当于西方童话中的精灵与食人妖。
